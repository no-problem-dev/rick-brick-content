### Summary and Outlook

The overarching trend today is the "synchronization of AI implementation and governance." The US government's national legislative framework and corporate organizational transformation reports confirm that AI has moved beyond the experimental phase and is becoming deeply embedded in society at an infrastructural level. The acceleration of AI in life sciences and finance, in particular, is creating new paradigms in both data volume and model accuracy. Conversely, the reality of climate change impacting even fundamental physical processes like Earth's rotation strongly warns of the urgent need to use technologies like AI for adaptation to extreme weather and optimization of energy efficiency. Going forward, how to harmonize "AI intelligence" with "real-world physics and institutions" will be key to determining national competitiveness.
