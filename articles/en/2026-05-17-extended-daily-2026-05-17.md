#### Energy Engineering and Climate Science
Cement production is a significant global source of CO2 emissions, but a radical reduction method was announced by the American Chemical Society (ACS). This approach successfully reduced input energy by 70% and suppressed CO2 emissions by up to 98% compared to conventional heating processes.
