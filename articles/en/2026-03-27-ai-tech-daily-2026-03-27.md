### 2. Today's Highlights

#### Google DeepMind Releases Toolkit to Detect and Measure Harmful AI Manipulation
On March 26, 2026, Google DeepMind released the first validated toolkit for investigating the harmful manipulative effects (Harmful Manipulation) that AI models can have on humans. This research was initiated out of concern that as AI models gain more natural conversational abilities, the risk of them deceptively guiding human thoughts and behaviors is increasing.

The research team conducted nine studies involving over 10,000 participants in the UK, US, and India, scientifically analyzing how AI can influence human decision-making. The released toolkit is designed to allow general researchers to replicate and utilize the methods employed in this study. This will enable AI developers to more clearly define the boundary between "beneficial persuasion" and "harmful manipulation" and implement safety measures. The company plans to expand its research scope to include how images, audio, and agent capabilities contribute to AI's manipulative potential. This will be a crucial milestone in ensuring the reliability of AI in its societal implementation.
[Source: Google DeepMind Official Blog "Protecting People from Harmful Manipulation"](https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/protecting-people-from-harmful-manipulation/)

#### Google Search "Live" Feature Launches in Over 200 Countries and Regions Worldwide
On March 26, 2026, Google announced the global rollout of "Search Live" across all languages and regions, powered by Gemini 3.1 Flash Live. Through the Google app, users can engage in real-time conversations using voice and camera. This feature goes beyond simple text search, allowing AI to understand visual context and respond instantly to the user's immediate situation.

Gemini 3.1 Flash Live is designed to deliver extremely low latency and natural conversations, with enhanced multilingual support. For example, if a user points their camera at a piece of furniture they don't know how to assemble and asks, "How do I put this together?", the AI will analyze the image and provide real-time assembly guidance. The global deployment of this technology marks a decisive step towards AI becoming a "personal intelligence" deeply integrated into individuals' daily lives. It is transforming the concept of traditional search engines into AI becoming an immediate problem-solving partner in all aspects of life.
