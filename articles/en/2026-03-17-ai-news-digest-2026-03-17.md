#### NVIDIA Fully Launches 'AI Computing for Space'
At GTC 2026, NVIDIA announced a new accelerated computing platform designed for AI operations in space. The newly unveiled "NVIDIA Space-1 Vera Rubin Module," along with the related IGX Thor and Jetson Orin platforms, deliver data center-class performance and edge inference capabilities in extremely Size, Weight, and Power (SWaP) constrained environments.

This technology enables AI applications beyond terrestrial limitations, such as in-orbit data centers, geospatial intelligence, and autonomous spacecraft operations. Key players in the space industry, including Aetherflux and Axiom Space, have already begun adopting these platforms. By shifting AI data processing from "ground-based data centers" to "the data source itself (space)," the expectation is to reduce downlink dependency and significantly enhance mission autonomy and real-time capabilities.
