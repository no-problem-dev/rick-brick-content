With the widespread adoption of AVs, commuters can choose to avoid expensive parking fees in city centers and opt for autonomous parking in suburban areas. This suggests the possibility of converting vast parking spaces in urban centers into residential or commercial facilities, highlighting the economic impact of AI adoption on urban planning.

#### Robotics & Autonomous Agents
RoboTwin, a Czech startup, has unveiled technology that allows industrial robots to acquire new skills without needing to be programmed, by intuitively teaching them the movements of skilled workers. This is an innovation that significantly lowers the barrier to automation for small and medium-sized manufacturers that cannot afford dedicated programming staff. By combining AI and computer vision, this method allows robots to copy human demonstrations, advancing the democratization of automation in manufacturing.

#### Computational Social Science
The National University of Singapore (NUS) has established the "Computational Social Science and Humanities (CSSH)" center to integrate data science and AI with social sciences and humanities. This center is developing a platform that utilizes LLMs to model diverse public personas and test/scrutinize the impact of policy interventions before they are actually implemented. By simulating complex social phenomena, it aims to foster the formation of more inclusive and resilient societal foundations.
