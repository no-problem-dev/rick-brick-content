### 2. Today's Highlights

#### Anthropic Announces Public Beta for Claude Security
Anthropic released the public beta of "Claude Security" for cybersecurity professional teams on April 30, 2026 (local time). This tool, powered by their latest model "Opus 4.7," enables automated scanning for vulnerabilities within complex codebases in large organizations and generates appropriate patches using generative AI's reasoning capabilities.

Evolved from the previous research preview (Claude Code Security), it includes periodic scanning, explanation and management features for detection results, and Markdown export functionality for integration with standard auditing systems. This product is offered as part of Anthropic's enterprise subscription, "Claude Enterprise." The background to this release is the rapid acceleration of development speed due to AI-driven code generation, which conversely increases new attack surfaces. Anthropic is also accelerating the formation of an ecosystem for defensive AI by collaborating with security partners such as CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, and Wiz.

Source: [Anthropic News: Claude Security Public Beta](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-security-public-beta)

#### OpenAI Introduces "Advanced Account Security"
OpenAI began offering "Advanced Account Security" for ChatGPT and Codex users on April 30, 2026. This is an opt-in setting for individuals at high risk of digital attacks or those seeking enhanced security.

The core of this feature is strengthened phishing-resistant authentication, including the preferential offering of hardware security key (YubiKey) bundles through a partnership with Yubico. Notably, enabling this setting automatically guarantees that conversational data sent from the account will not be used for model training. This represents a significant step towards balancing security and privacy for enterprises and professionals handling sensitive information. OpenAI states its commitment to continuously enhancing user control over their privacy and security as AI becomes more integrated into personal lives and high-stakes operations.

Source: [OpenAI Blog: Introducing Advanced Account Security](https://openai.com/index/introducing-advanced-account-security/)
